Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for peak season travel (June-September). For off-season adventures, 2-3 months advance booking is usually sufficient.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For certain destinations, we offer flexible payment plans.

Do you offer customized travel itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized travel experiences. Contact our team with your preferences, and we'll craft a unique journey just for you.

What's your cancellation policy?

Our standard policy allows for full refunds up to 45 days before departure. Cancellations within 30-45 days receive a 50% refund. Special terms may apply for certain packages.
