About Innovatia Travel

Explore the world with us - we're your gateway to extraordinary experiences and unforgettable adventures.

Our Story

Innovatia Travel was born from a deep passion for exploration and a desire to share the world's hidden gems with adventurous travelers. Founded in 2015 by a group of travel enthusiasts, we've grown from a small boutique agency to a leading provider of authentic travel experiences.

Our journey began with specialized tours to the Azores, where we developed deep connections with local communities and discovered the untold stories of this magical archipelago. Today, we've expanded our horizons to offer immersive experiences across the globe, while maintaining our commitment to sustainable tourism and authentic cultural exchange.

What sets us apart is our dedication to creating journeys that transform travelers, not just transport them. We believe that travel is about making meaningful connections - with places, people, and cultures that enrich our understanding of the world.

Our Philosophy

Authentic Experiences

We believe travel should be immersive and genuine. Our itineraries take you beyond tourist traps to experience destinations through local eyes.

Sustainable Tourism

We're committed to preserving the places we love. Our trips minimize environmental impact and support local communities.

Personal Touch

Every journey is unique. We tailor experiences to your interests, ensuring your adventure is exactly what you dreamed of.
